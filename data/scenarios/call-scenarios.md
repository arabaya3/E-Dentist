# Voice Engine Call Scenarios

## 1. Appointment Confirmation (EN)
- Agent: Confirm hygiene visit time and provider.
- Expected intents: confirm, reschedule, cancel.
- Sample prompt: "Call Mrs. Lopez to confirm tomorrow's cleaning at 09:00, offer to reschedule if needed."

## 2. Treatment Follow-up (AR)
- Agent: Check pain levels after root canal.
- Expected intents: stable, pain, medication request.
- Sample prompt: "Call the patient two days after the root canal to ask about their pain level and whether they need additional pain relief."

## 3. Insurance Clarification (EN)
- Agent: Gather insurance ID.
- Expected intents: provide data, request callback.
- Sample prompt: "Collect the member ID and date of birth for claim submission."

## 4. Payment Reminder (AR)
- Agent: Remind of outstanding balance.
- Expected intents: pay now, request plan, dispute.
- Sample prompt: "Remind the patient that 320 SAR remains due for their last visit and offer to arrange a payment plan if needed."

## 5. Emergency Triage (Bilingual)
- Agent: Determine urgency and route call.
- Expected intents: emergency, schedule visit, home care.
- Sample prompt: "Start in Arabic, switch to English if caller does, capture symptoms within 30 seconds."
